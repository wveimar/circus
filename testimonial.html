    Un lugar realmente fascinante: uno de los pocos donde puedes ver instalaciones auténticas, una colección inmensa de carpas y carteles impresionantes.

  • Robert V. Maguire - Nevada

    Quién iba a imaginar que Baraboo jugó un papel tan importante en la historia del circo en EE. UU. La historia es fascinante: aquí comenzó el show en 1884 y permaneció hasta 1918.

  • Kenneth L. Miller - Winsconsin

    There are several attractions at Zooks. They have a beautiful newer building where they are restoring many old Zooks Circus from all over the world. If you are up for a walk there are old barns - one is full of costumes.

  • Wilma J. Jackson - Oklahoma

    This is an important part of American History - the travelling circuses. For children, it is an eye-opening experience that they cannot get in a classroom. For adults, it is a look back at times before computer games and Smartphones.
